Baking Ovens

Baking ovens are used for baking bread, cakes, and other baked goods. They are heated by gas, electricity, or wood, and can be found in homes, bakeries, and restaurants. Baking ovens come in many shapes and sizes, from small countertop models to large commercial ovens.
