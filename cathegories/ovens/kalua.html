🌍 Kalua Oven

The Kalua oven is a traditional Hawaiian underground oven used for slow-roasting meat, especially during luaus. The cooking process gives the food a rich, smoky flavor.

💡 Fun Fact: "Kalua" means "to cook in an underground oven" in Hawaiian.

🔥 Cooking Method: Underground roasting using hot stones and banana leaves.

🍖 Famous Dish: Kalua Pig - a slow-cooked, tender, and smoky Hawaiian delicacy.
