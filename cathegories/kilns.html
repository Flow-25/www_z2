Kilns

Kilns are used for firing ceramics, glass, and other materials at high temperatures. They are commonly used in pottery studios, glassblowing workshops, and industrial manufacturing plants. Kilns come in many different shapes and sizes, from small tabletop models to large industrial kilns.
