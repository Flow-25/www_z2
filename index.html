The Evolution of Ovens

Since the dawn of civilization, ovens have played a vital role in cooking, industry, and craftsmanship. From the simplest earth ovens used by ancient societies to the cutting-edge industrial ovens of today, these heating chambers have shaped the way we prepare food, manufacture goods, and create art.
